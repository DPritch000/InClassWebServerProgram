How To Play

  1. Sign in at the event registration and get your role and band (if required).
  2. Humans complete missions and avoid Zombies until mission end.
  3. Zombies attempt to tag Humans to increase their numbers.
  4. Follow mission-specific rules (time, location, objectives).
  5. Report tags and follow marshal instructions for any disputes.
