Official Rules

  • Players are assigned Humans or Zombies at the start.
  • Zombies tag Humans by touching them — tagged Humans become Zombies.
  • Humans may use designated safe zones; tagging is not allowed within them.
  • No physical force; harassment or dangerous behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Follow event marshals' instructions for safety and fairness.
